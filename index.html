Tiago Machado

At a glance

I'm an AI Engineer, and I like to develop systems for enhancing human design activities. My recent works combine machine learning, backend development, and data science in fields such as decision-making simulations, games, edutech, and agrotech.

New York University

I did my PhD in the Computer Science and Enginnering department at NYU Tandon. I worked at the NYU Game Innovation Lab and my thesis discuss the design, development and evaluation of AI-Game-Design assistant systems.

Federal University of Pernambuco (Brazil)

I have done my Masters and my Bachelors at UFPE. My thesis is related to the design of tools to improve the communication between game designers and game engineers.

What I do

Here I list some of my past and current projects. Most of them are about AI and machine learning, backend development, and game programming.

Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning

During the Omdena-Agreed Earth project, I worked on the design of a knowledge guided machine learning model to predict nitrous dioxide fluxes.

Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning/Backend Development

During my time at Northeastern University, I worked on the design of machine learning models for decision making simulations, and interactive design assistance. The models are part of the StudyCrafter.com plataform

Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning

During the course of my PhD, I have designed systems that incorporate AI into the game design process. Click on the figure to see a demo.

Relational Databases

I developed a project for storing, replaying and querying events in gameplay sessions. Although the amount of events happening in a game can be pretty intense, a relational data base could handle well the write/read requests in this context.

Games

I developed games when working at a pioneer EdTech project in Brazil. Click on the images to download the games and watch their videos.

Visualization

I designed a visualization tool for a tweeter hashtag sentiment analysis and for visualizing heatmaps of game elements.
